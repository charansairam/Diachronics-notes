The Diachronics notes is a collection of some pointers from the discussions I have with people working on Dravidian, and more broadly historical and comparative linguistics in the subcontinent today, along with material composed by those who worked on it in the past.

This repository is now published as a Quartz site while remaining editable as plain Markdown in Obsidian. Cloning the repository and opening it as an Obsidian vault is still the best way to work on the notes directly.
